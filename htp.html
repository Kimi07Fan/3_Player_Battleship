How to play

This project is an extension of the classic game, Battleship. Rules are the same as the
original game, with the exception of the added player, but for those playing for the first time, here is a basic set of rules:-

1. There will be three boards on the screen, yours is on the left, the other players' is on the right and the computers' is on the bottom.

2. First, however, you must set up your own board. Select the ship tokens on the left one by one and align them using the 'change orientation'
button on the panel on left of the screen. Your current orientation is also displayed on the same panel.

3. Click the ships one by one and select where the starting point for the ship must be. Once you've put all 4 ships then you're ready to go!

4. Your objective in the match is to sink all the ships of both the other player and the computer before they sink yours!
